Goiânia, Goiás, Brasil
Investigador Nacional Nivel 1, SNI, México

I'm currently a research fellow at the Universidade Federal de Goiás (UFG), Brazil, where I'm also a collaborating professor at the Ecology and Evolution Graduate Program. I'm affiliated to the amazing group of José Alexandre Diniz-Filho, working on macroecological theory and methods integrating macroecology with phylogenetic approaches to understand geographic patterns of biodiversity.
