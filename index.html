Dr. Fabricio Villalobos Research fellow Laboratório de Ecologia Teórica e Síntese Universidade Federal de Goiás

I'm affiliated to the amazing group of José Alexandre Diniz-Filho, working on macroecological theory and methods integrating macroecology with phylogenetic approaches to understand geographic patterns of biodiversity.
